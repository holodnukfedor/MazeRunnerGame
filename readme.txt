WPF C# game.

Game posibilities:
	-animated sprites;
	-random maze generating;
	-player moves and collects coins (they are generating in random places with period 5c);
	-zombie opponents (at the beginning of the game is moving random route 1 enemy, after 10 coins - 2 enemies, after 30 coins - enemies move to the player on the optimal route.);
	-results: time of the game, the number of coins the player's name - in XML.
